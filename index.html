About

Profile

PROFESSOR ENEFIOK EFFIONG ESSIEN SAN, FCArb, PhD (Birmingham); Ed.D (h.c., Belize): Commonwealth Study Fellow (1995-1998); Professor of Property and Commercial Law (2001 to date); Dean, Faculty of Law (14 years: 2000-2009, 2012 - 2015); Dean of Deans (i.e., Chairman Committee of Deans), 2012 - 2015; Member, Governing Council of Legal Education (2000-.2009, 2012-2015); Member, Nigerian Institute of Advanced Legal Studies Governing Council (2012-2015); Member, Governing Council of the University of Uyo (2013 to 2020); Founding President, Society for International Humanitarian Law Teachers (2011 to 2016); MNIM, FCAI, FIIA, FIPDM, FCPM, FCIL, FCArb, Barrister-at-law (1985 to date); Notary Public for Nigeria (1993 to date); Vice Chancellor, University of Uyo 2015 to 2020; Senior Advocate of Nigeria – SAN (2017 to date)
